— De la rédaction — Dispatches from the front lines of French grammar. Ten verbs per round, two pronouns apiece — one singular, one plural, drawn from different persons, as the rules of polite drilling demand. The Mixed Drill pits four verbs against four tenses; survivors are entitled to a café crème and a knowing glance. Accents are forgiven — these are difficult times — but we shall note them all the same. — signé, la Rédaction du Conjugueur
